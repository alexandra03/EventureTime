About Eventure Time

Ever wondered what's going on around you? Looking to take an awesome instagram picture to make all of your follwers jealous of your social life?

Eventure Time is an online platform that allows you to find any event near you based on your interest. Simply login to Facebook and instantaneously see events you might be interested in.

Don't see an event that you're interested in? Search for a variety of different events such as networking events, concerts, sporting events and many more!

Found an event you like but don't have a way of getting there? We've got you covered. With the click of a button, you can get an Uber to your desired event.
